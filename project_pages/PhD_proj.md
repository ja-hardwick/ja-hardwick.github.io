*Completed December 2025*

My PhD project explored how invasive non-native plants drive river geomorphic change by disrupting riverbank stability, augementing erosion and sediment dynamics. 
I used a combination of field data, piecewise strucutral equation modelling, lab experiments and landscape evolution modeling to reveal the impacts of plant invasions on riverbank processes.  

Funded by a Newcastle University studentship.
